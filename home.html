ABOUT

Hi, I'm Piya. I am passionate about creating the best technology experience for all users through practice of human-centered design. I enjoy creating creative and colourful solutions for all kinds of problems.
Throughout my degree I have learnt about and applied practical knowledge of user experience, interaction design, and web development. I have taken part in many UI/UX and front-end development projects, where I have moved through the stages of user research, wireframing, prototyping, and testing. I have learnt to work in and lead a team effectively, ensure project goals are met and assess progress, and incorporate usability principles and best practice.
University of Melbourne graduate with a Bachelor in Design (Major in Digital Technologies).
Currently studying my Master of Information Technology at the University of Melbourne.
My dream is to collaborate with an open-minded, passionate team, who are always finding new applications of usability thinking. I am excited to have a career that offers the opportunity to make positive impact in peoples lives through their interactions with technology.
